Overview

Overview

Truecoach is a software platform designed to facilitate seamless communication between fitness coaches and their clients, while also enabling efficient progress tracking and workout management. The platform offers distinct functionalities for both coaches and clients. Some of its key features include:

  • Messaging: Coaches can easily communicate with their clients through the app, fostering constant support and guidance.
  • Progress Tracking: The software allows for comprehensive monitoring of clients' progress, helping coaches make data-driven decisions.
  • Video Uploads: Clients can upload videos showcasing their workout results, which enhances feedback and analysis from their coaches.
  • Workout Results Recording: Clients can effortlessly log their workout results within the app, simplifying the process of progress documentation.

Goal of this Redesign

The goal of this redesign is to evaluate the client side of the Truecoach mobile app, and use design thinking to take advantage of any additional opportunities, and address any usability issues.

Problem Statement

Fitness enthusiasts need a way to accurately track their workouts each week, so they may accurately measure progress over time.
