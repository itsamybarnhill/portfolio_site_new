About

My Story

I am a former property manager turned UX Professional! In my career, I’ve always enjoyed solving complex problems, digging down to find other’s “why”, and fusing high level, objective thinking with practicality to create the best outcome possible. My goal in my user experience career is to use best research and design practices to uncover new opportunities for organizations, while creating empowering experiences for users.

I recently graduated from Career Foundry, a comprehensive UX Design bootcamp, where I gained practical experience in all stages of the design process, from research and ideation to prototyping and user testing. Figma is my design tool of choice, and I also have experience coding with HTML and CSS. I am seeking opportunities to work as part of a team in a collaborative environment where I can leverage my skills to make a positive impact and drive user satisfaction.

In my spare time, I enjoy powerlifting and I am also an equestrian!

I am currently located in Groton, CT and open to remote, contract or hybrid work opportunities!
